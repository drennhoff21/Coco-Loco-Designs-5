Based out of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, and shipping nation-wide, we offer customizable recreations of your furry loved ones or favorite Pop Culture characters, using layered scrapbook paper and mounted on fabric backgrounds.


Get to know the girl behind the name! Coco is a 10 year old French Bulldog from Queens, New York. She loves naps, cheese, and whatever you happen to be eating.

Her personality and striking good looks inspired her owner to make several portraits of her. It caught the eye of several admirers and thus a company was born.
